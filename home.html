Why Should We Recycle?

Protect the Environment

Recycling reduces pollution by minimizing the need for new raw materials. It helps save the planet from further degradation.

Conserve Resources

Recycling saves valuable resources like water, minerals, and energy. It helps conserve natural ecosystems and habitats.

Save Energy

Recycling products such as metals, glass, and plastics uses far less energy compared to making them from scratch.

Fun Facts About Recycling

Recycling one ton of paper saves 17 trees!

Recycling aluminum saves 95% of the energy compared to making new aluminum.

The average American produces about 4.4 pounds of trash every day.
